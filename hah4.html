A few days passed after that day under the sakura tree.
Things slowly returned to normal.
But something had changed.
Sayori's friends seemed a little worried about what happened that day when she skipped class.
Still, Sayori herself seemed calmer.
And somehow…
She spent more time near me.

✿   ·   ✿
Classroom
Kazuki — POV

I was staring outside the window again.
The sky was clear.
Sometimes I wonder what it means to be someone special in another person's life.
Yesterday I watched an anime that talked about that.
It made me think.
While I was looking outside, I noticed something in the window's reflection.
Sayori.
She was looking at me.
I didn't turn around.
Because she probably didn't know I could see her through the reflection.
Then she spoke.
Sayori: "Kazuki…"
I turned slightly.
Kazuki: "Yeah, Sayori. What's up?"
She hesitated a little.
Sayori: "Do you have a phone?"
Kazuki: "I do. Why?"
Sayori: "No reason…"
She pauses for a moment.
Sayori: "Can we share contact information?"
That surprised me.
I didn't expect that.
Kazuki (thinking): That was unexpected.
Kazuki: "Oh… sure."
I took out my phone and showed her my contact.
Kazuki: "Here."
She quickly saved it.
For some reason, I realized something at that moment.
She might be the first real friend I have added on this app.

·   ·   ·
Lunch Break
Sayori — POV

I did it.
I got his contact number.
For some reason…
I feel happy.
I open his profile.
His display picture is a mountain view.
That fits him somehow.
Quiet.
Calm.
After what happened that day under the sakura tree, I feel like we became closer.
And strangely…
My interaction with my other friends became less.
Maybe they were never real friends anyway.
I look at the time.
It's lunch break.
I smile slightly.
Sayori (thinking):
Should I text him and ask where he is?
Then I shake my head.
Sayori: "Dummy."
It's lunch time.
He should be at the same place as always.
Under the pink sakura tree.
