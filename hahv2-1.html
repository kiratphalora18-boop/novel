Him and Her · Volume Two
Chapter One

Why Him?

✿
Kazuki — POV

A few months have passed.
Somehow, during that time, Sayori became someone I can call a friend.
Or at least… I think so.
Most of our conversations only happen in class.
I still don't know why she asked for my contact number that day.
We rarely text.
But still…
Since that rainy day when we shared the umbrella, something feels different.
Sometimes I find myself thinking about it.
About her.
About that moment.

I don't really understand this feeling.
Maybe it's just curiosity.
Yeah.
Curiosity.
I should probably talk to her about it.
Right now, she's sitting in the seat next to me.
Like always.
Quiet.
Zoned out in her own thoughts.

✿   ·   ✿
Sayori — POV

He's right next to me.
And yet…
I can't even look him in the eyes.
Why?
I don't know.
Maybe I'll start blushing.
Maybe I'll say something embarrassing.
I still don't understand it myself.

Why him?
Why did I fall for Kazuki?
Maybe…
It's because he sees me for who I really am.
The real me.
Not the girl everyone watches in class.
But the person behind that.
Yes.
He sees that.
But still…
What should I do?

Someone suddenly calls my name.
Rika: "Sayori!"
I snap out of my thoughts.
Sayori: "Oh… sorry, Rika. I zoned out."
Rika laughs a little.
Rika: "You always do that. It's normal."
She leans closer.
Rika: "So… what were you thinking about?"
Sayori: "Eh? Me? Nothing."
Rika gives me a suspicious look.
Rika: "Oh, come on…"
Before she can continue—
The lunch bell rings.
Rika stands up quickly.
Rika: "It's lunch. Let's go, Sayori."
I glance once toward Kazuki.
Then I quietly stand up.

· · ·
Cafeteria — Lunch Time
Sayori — POV

Rika sits across from me, staring with curiosity.
Rika: "So, Sayori… what have you been thinking about lately?"
She leans forward with a teasing smile.
Rika: "I'm really curious to know."
Rika: "Sayoriiii…"
I look a little nervous.
Sayori: "Eh… I… um… what should we eat?"
Rika blinks.
Rika: "What?"
She folds her arms.
Rika: "Come on. Don't change the topic."
Rika: "What's gotten into you, Sayori?"

I hesitate.
Then I quietly speak.
Sayori: "Rika…"
Sayori: "I think… I've fallen for someone."
Rika's eyes widen.
Rika: "WHAT?!"
A few students nearby turn their heads.
Rika: "Sayori, did you hit your head or something?"
Sayori: "No. I'm serious."
Rika leans closer.
Rika: "Okay… okay…"
Rika: "So who is it?"

I pause.
My mind starts racing.
Should I tell her?
Should I say his name?
Or should I hide it…?
I take a small breath.
Why hide it?
Sayori: "It's… Kazuki."
Rika freezes for a moment.
Rika: "Wait… what?"
Rika: "Kazuki? That quiet boy?"
I quickly look around.
Sayori: "Don't say it so loudly!"
Rika studies my face for a moment.
Then she slowly smiles.
Rika: "Well…"
Rika: "It's your decision."
Sayori: "What do you mean?"
Rika leans back in her chair.
Rika: "I'll help you."
Sayori: "Help me?"
Rika gives a mischievous grin.
Rika: "Hehehe…"

That was a very evil smile from Rika.
✿   ·   ✿
Pink Sakura Tree
Kazuki — POV

The sakura tree stands quietly like always.
Sometimes I wonder…
How long has it been standing here?
How many students have sat under it?
How many stories has it watched?
And now…
It's watching ours.

— End of Chapter One —
