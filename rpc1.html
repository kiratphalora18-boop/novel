The Rain Pact
Chapter One

Drizzle and Blue Umbrellas

🌧️

The first time Aoi met him, she didn't even know his name.
It was raining—of course it was. The kind of drizzle that crept in slowly, catching everyone off guard. Students huddled under awnings, jackets over their heads, grumbling.
Aoi, on the other hand, stood silently beneath the cherry blossom tree just outside the school gate, her bag held tightly to her chest, hair already damp.
She hated the rain.
Not because she got wet. But because it reminded her of everything she tried to forget.

Then came the voice.
"Hey," he said softly. "You'll catch a cold standing like that."
She turned. A boy with messy hair, untucked uniform, and a calm smile held out a deep blue umbrella.
"You're in Class 2-B, right? Want to share?"
Aoi blinked. "I'm okay."
But the boy just chuckled and stepped beside her anyway.
"Too bad. I'm not okay with seeing someone left alone in the rain."

They walked together without another word.
At the next corner, she expected him to turn—but he didn't.
He walked her all the way home.
And before she could ask for his name...
He was gone.

The next day, it rained again.
And there he was, standing under the same tree, blue umbrella in hand.
"Good morning," he said. "Looks like it's our thing now."
She didn't know it then, but the boy with the umbrella would become her calm in every storm.
