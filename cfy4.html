🔍 Case File: You · Chapter 4

One More

Five murders. One symbol. And a figure that shouldn't exist.

Mystery Drama Action By AKA

It was 7:00 AM.

Sato had barely opened his eyes when his phone suddenly rang beside him.

Akane.

Still half asleep, he answered the call.

"Sato," Akane said immediately, her voice unusually tense. "Get to the agency as fast as you can."

The line disconnected.

Sato stared at the screen for a second before quickly getting dressed.

Something had happened.

🏢
Destination: The Agency Arrived · ~20 minutes later

About twenty minutes later, Sato rushed into the agency building.

Akane was already waiting near the entrance, arms folded tightly.

The expression on her face told him everything.

Sato frowned. "What's the issue?"

Akane looked directly at him.

"There's been another murder."

Sato stopped walking.

"…Another one?"

Akane nodded slowly.

"We need to head to the crime scene now."

Sato exhaled sharply. "Yeah. Let's go."

🏪
Crime Scene: Local Store, City Edge Isolated · No CCTV · No nearby buildings

The crime scene was located near the edge of the city.

A small local store stood alone beside an empty road, surrounded by silence.

No crowds. No nearby buildings. Just old electric poles and the sound of wind brushing through trees.

Sato stepped out of the car and looked around carefully.

"This place is isolated," he muttered. "Barely any people around."

His eyes narrowed toward the shop entrance.

"And no CCTV cameras either."

Akane glanced toward the damaged counter inside the store.

"At first glance, it could pass as a robbery."

Sato nodded slightly. "Yeah… maybe."

Victim · Crime Scene
Victim #5
Homicide
Location Near the register
Scene Staged as robbery
Month Five in thirty days

Inside the store, the victim's body lay motionless near the register.

Akane crouched beside it, examining the wounds carefully.

Then suddenly —

"Sato."

Her voice became colder.

"Look at this."

Sato walked over.

The moment he saw it, his expression darkened instantly.

· · ·

Three lines. A triangle. A bleeding eye.

Again.

Sato stared at it in silence.

"Five murders…" he said quietly. "Five murders in the same month."

His jaw tightened.

"And every single one marked with that symbol."

He looked around the empty shop once more.

"So this wasn't just a robbery after all."

🗂 The Symbol — All Five Scenes
01 Three lines forming a triangle
02 A bleeding eye at the center
03 Present at every murder this month
?? A signature — or a message?

Akane stood up slowly. "What now?"

Sato turned toward her.

"Send the body for further examination."

Then he glanced toward the dark corners of the store.

"And search the area. Anything suspicious, anything unusual — I want every detail."

Akane nodded. "Understood."

As officers began moving around the scene, Sato slowly walked deeper into the store, scanning everything carefully.

Broken shelves. Scattered items. A faint metallic smell in the air.

Then —

Movement.

👤
A black figure — back entrance. Tall. Motionless. Watching · Then gone · No trace left behind

For a split second, Sato saw a black figure standing outside the back entrance.

Tall. Motionless. Watching him.

Sato's eyes widened slightly.

"What the —"

The figure vanished.

Sato immediately turned toward the exit.

"Hey!"

But by the time he reached outside…

There was nobody there.

· · ·

Only silence.

And the cold morning wind.
