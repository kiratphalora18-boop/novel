The Boy Who Photographed the Sky

Romance Drama Sad By AKA
7:03 AM
617

Photos of the sky · All named 7:03 AM

Ren only took pictures of the sky.

Not people, not places — just the sky.

Every morning before school, he'd sit on the rooftop of his apartment complex, camera slung around his neck, staring upward like the clouds held answers he wasn't brave enough to ask for.

On his phone, in a folder buried deep within hundreds of others, were exactly 617 photos of the sky — all named with the same date and time:

"7:03 AM."

No one knew why.

"Dude, you're weird," his classmate had once laughed.
"All these cloud photos… what are you trying to find up there?"

Ren didn't answer.

Because he wasn't looking for anything.

He was waiting.

· · ·

That same morning — the one that started like every other — was when she appeared.

The girl with paper-white skin, messy hair tied with a ribbon too red to ignore, and the softest voice he'd ever heard.

She stood at the front of the classroom, fingers clenched tightly around a leather journal, and introduced herself without looking up.

"…I'm Sayo."

That was all.
No last name. No hometown. Just a name.
Just a voice that sounded like it came from somewhere far away.

The teacher pointed to the empty seat beside Ren.

And just like that, the boy who looked at the sky met the girl who never looked up.

· · ·

She never spoke first.
Never joined lunch groups.
And never let anyone touch the brown journal she always carried — not even the teachers.

But Ren watched her from the corner of his eye, quiet and curious.

She always wore long sleeves, even when the weather burned.
Her fingers trembled slightly when she wrote.
And every time someone laughed too loudly, she flinched — just a little.

· · ·

That night, Ren took another picture of the sky.

It was cloudy.

Like it was hiding something.

I sat beside him today.
The boy who looks at the sky like it can heal something broken.
I think he's going to ruin me.
Or maybe… I'll ruin him first.
