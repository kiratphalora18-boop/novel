Him and Her
Chapter Two

Normal Day?

✿

Yesterday was… something.
At least, that is what I told myself.
But today should be normal again.
Nothing new.
Except for the new seating arrangement.
I arrived at school a little early today.
When I entered the classroom, no one was there yet.
Honestly, I do not mind that.
A quiet classroom is peaceful.
I sat down at my seat and was about to take out a book to read when—
She walked in.
Sayori.
I did not expect her to arrive this early.
She noticed me and smiled.
Sayori: "Oh, Kazuki… good morning."
Kazuki: "Good morning."
She looked a little surprised.
Sayori: "So you beat me today, huh?"
Kazuki: "What?"
Sayori: "Usually I am the first one to arrive. But today you were here before me."
Kazuki: "Oh… I see."
After that…
There was silence.

Soon the classroom slowly filled with other students, and the day started like usual.
Classes went on.
Nothing special happened.
Then it was finally lunch break.
Like always, I quietly left the classroom.
But this time…
Sayori noticed.
She had seen me leave the classroom during lunch before.
And today—
She decided to follow me.

✿   ·   ✿
Kazuki — POV

It is not like the classroom bothers me.
But this place is better.
Near the small vending machines, beside the pink sakura tree.
It is quiet here.
Almost no one comes here during lunch.
I like it.
Just as I was about to sit down—
A voice interrupted me.
Sayori: "Ohhh… so this is where you come."
I turn around.
Kazuki: "Sayori? What are you doing here?"
She walks closer, looking around the place with curiosity.
Sayori: "Me? I was curious… so I followed you."
Kazuki: "Curious… about me?"
She smiles slightly.
And for some reason…
That made me even more confused.

·   ·   ·
Sayori — POV

I was just curious.
Every day during lunch, Kazuki quietly leaves the classroom and disappears somewhere.
No one seems to notice.
But I did.
So today… I followed him.
And the place he comes to is this.
A quiet spot near the vending machines.
A bench under a pink sakura tree.
The petals slowly fall with the wind.
It is… beautiful.
Now I understand why he comes here.

Kazuki: "So… that's the reason?"
Sayori: "Yeah, you could say that."
He looks a little confused but sits on the bench.
Kazuki: "What about your friends? Do they know you are here?"
I shrug slightly.
Sayori: "Don't mind them."
I look around the peaceful area.
Sayori: "It's so peaceful here."
Kazuki: "Sure it is."
For a moment, we both stay quiet.
Then I ask something I was thinking about.
Sayori: "In the classroom, everyone seems to be having fun. Don't you have friends, Kazuki?"
He pauses before answering.
Kazuki: "I do… and I don't."
I raise an eyebrow.
Sayori: "Don't lie."
He shakes his head slightly.
Kazuki: "I'm not lying. I just don't like being the center of attraction… or attention."
I look down for a second.
Sayori: "Oh… same."

He looks surprised.
Sayori: "I don't want the unnecessary attention people give me. It's uncomfortable."
I sigh quietly.
Sayori: "Honestly… I hate it sometimes."
A few sakura petals fall around us.
Sayori: "I wish I had something like you have."
Kazuki looks confused.
I can almost see the question in his eyes.
Kazuki: "Oh… I see now."
He gestures toward the bench and the tree.
Kazuki: "Well, whenever you want some peace… you can come here."
Kazuki: "It's a peaceful place."
I smile.
Sayori: "Sure it is."
Then I look at him again.
Sayori: "And hey… you do have a friend."
Kazuki blinks.
Kazuki: "I do?"
I smile a little wider.
Sayori: "Did you forget already?"
I point at myself.
Sayori: "It's me."
For a moment, he looks surprised.
Then he smiles.
Kazuki: "Oh… right."
Kazuki: "I do have a friend."
Kazuki: "You."
The pink petals continue falling around us.
And for the first time…
It feels like we are not from different worlds anymore.

— End of Chapter Two —
