Chapter Eight

Him

🌸
Sayori — POV

As I was walking toward the place where Kazuki usually spends lunch…
I saw someone familiar.
Aoi.
My childhood friend.
I knew she studied at the same high school as me, but we were not in the same class.
It happens sometimes.
We stopped and talked for a few minutes.
Just small things.
Before leaving, she handed me her umbrella.
Aoi: "Take it. It's going to rain soon."
I looked up at the sky.
The clouds were slowly getting darker.
I thanked her and continued walking.
I was in a bit of a hurry.
Kazuki was probably already there.

✿   ·   ✿
Pink Sakura Tree Bench

When I arrived…
He was there.
Sitting on the bench.
Reading quietly like always.
For a moment, I just stood there and watched him.
His world feels different from mine.
Calm.
Peaceful.
I hope…
He really sees me as a friend.
I slowly walked closer.
He noticed me and looked up.
Kazuki: "Oh… you brought an umbrella."

At that moment…
The rain started falling.
Soft at first.
Then a little stronger.
We opened the umbrella and stood close together under it.
It was quiet.
Only the sound of rain and the wind moving the sakura leaves.
Being this close felt strange.
I could even hear his breathing.
For some reason…
My heart started beating faster.
And then I realized something.
Something I hadn't noticed before.
Maybe…
I have already fallen for him.

— End of Chapter Eight —
