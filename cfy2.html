🔍 Case File: You · Chapter 2

The Clue

The report was wrong. Someone made sure of it.

Crime Thriller By AKA

The chief called both Sato and Akane into his office.

The room was unusually quiet.

Chief Hayashi sat behind his desk, fingers crossed, his expression far more serious than usual. The moment they entered, Sato could already tell —

This case had become bigger than they thought.

The chief looked at them for a moment before speaking.

"In the past two months, four murders have taken place. Including today's victim."

He slid several files across the desk.
"Same pattern. Same symbol."

Sato nodded slowly. "Yes, Chief. I believe an organization… or maybe even a cult, is behind this."

The chief sighed.
"The problem is — we don't know what we're dealing with."

He pushed the files closer toward Sato.
"Sato, I want you in charge of investigating all four cases. Everything related to these murders is now under your supervision."

Sato picked up the files.
"And Akane will assist you."

Akane blinked slightly. "Chief, I'm still working on another case—"
"Don't worry about it. Kento will take over from here. Right now, this investigation takes priority."

Akane gave a small nod. "Understood."

· · ·

A few minutes later, both of them returned to their desks with the files in hand.

Akane sat down first.
"I think we should start reviewing the victim reports."

Sato leaned back in his chair and sighed.
"What a drag…"

Akane shot him an annoyed glance.
Sato smirked faintly before opening one of the files.

Post Investigation Report FILE #0042 · RESTRICTED
Victim NameRuby Zeni
SexFemale
Age23
Observation

The victim's body was discovered inside a bathroom with no clothes on. Multiple bruises and dark marks were found across the body.

Conclusion

Evidence suggests the victim was sexually assaulted before death.

Status

⚠ Report incomplete — additional findings not updated.

Sato's expression slowly darkened.

"Akane… look at this."

Akane walked over and read the report carefully.
After a few seconds, she frowned.

"That's strange."
"What is?"
"I was present at the crime scene," she said quietly. "The body was in much worse condition than this report describes."

Sato looked back at the file.
Something wasn't right.

He closed it immediately and stood up.
"We need to go to the cold storage."
"Why?"
Sato grabbed his coat.
"I think I found our first clue."

As they walked through the dim hallway leading underground, Sato suddenly spoke.

"Whoever's doing this…"

🗣 Sato

"…is a devil disguised as a human."

Akane stayed silent.
For once, she agreed completely.

🧊
Cold Storage — Underground Freezing air · Sharp chemicals · Metal tables

A doctor stood near one of the metal tables, reviewing documents. He looked up as they approached.

"Ah, detectives."

Sato got straight to the point. "You handled Ruby Zeni's examination?"
"Yes."

The doctor adjusted his glasses.
"To be honest, the condition of the body was abnormal. There were multiple cuts, bruises, and signs of severe assault."

Akane narrowed her eyes.
"That wasn't included in the report."

The doctor hesitated.
"The body underwent additional examination afterward. The original report was never fully updated."

Sato's gaze sharpened instantly.
"And the additional findings?"

The doctor paused briefly before answering.

"We discovered traces of semen inside the victim's body."

Akane's eyes widened.
"What?"

Sato interrupted before she could continue.
"You still have the sample?"
"Yes."
"Good." Sato said immediately. "Run a DNA test."

🌧 Later that night

Rain tapped softly against the office windows.

Akane read the name once…
Then again.
Her eyes widened slightly.
"RIE?"

Sato nodded.
"The same RIE who works in the adult film industry."

Akane looked stunned.
"So we finally got a lead…"

Sato leaned back in his chair, a faint smile appearing on his face for the first time all day.

"Yeah."

His eyes darkened.

"And things are about to get ugly."
